Our Story

In the middle of our busy work schedules, we found ourselves stuck in the routine of fast food consumption. We wanted the comforting taste and nourishment of homemade meals. It was during one such moment of desire, that the spark of an idea ignited.




Two ambitious friends with a shared passion for wholesome, homemade cuisine, we started a journey to revolutionize the food delivery landscape. Our vision was simple yet profound: to bridge the gap between the convenience of fast food and the authenticity of homemade dishes.




In 2024, fueled by our desire to bring joy and nourishment to others, we founded Sunrise Today, our homemade food delivery service. Driven by our own experiences and a commitment to quality, we set out to connect talented chefs with thoughtful customers, all while using our own delivery team to ensure freshness and reliability.




From that humble beginning, our service has grown into a source of culinary delight, offering a diverse array of homemade dishes crafted with love and care. As we continue to grow, we remain dedicated to delivering not just meals but experiences that nourish the body and soul.




Join us on our journey as we redefine the way the world enjoys homemade goodness, one delicious bite at a time.
